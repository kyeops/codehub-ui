The "Inner-source" Movement

We are on a journey to institutionalize a culture of Inner-Sourcing across the Firm. We would like to introduce Stage – Booz Allen’s framework to help enable this transformation. It’s a lightweight framework with an intuitive UI that enables intelligent discovery of reusable Booz Allen developed software across distributed repositories.

  • Build for Re-use – develop smaller, modular open architecture software
  • Re-use – assemble software, accelerating delivery and focus on mission/business
  • Curate – internally crowd-source, maturing and innovating capabilities

Who Uses Stage?

Development Lead

I'm kicking off a new project and I need to establish a DevOps Pipeline solution. My client environment uses Jenkins and deploys to the government AWS cloud. Does Booz Allen have a template I can reuse and/or provision to get me started more quickly?

Analytics Consultant / Algorithm Developer

I need to quickly set up big data platform to support execution of algorithms for statistical analysis on large sets of cyber data. I am comfortable with Apache big data products such as Kafka, Accumulo and Storm. Is there a blueprint or preconfigured data platform stack I can reuse to get me started?

SDG / Market Lead

I want to understand the market landscape for business intelligence technologies deployed for our government clients so I can make informed financial, investment and training decisions to support our firm's software development staff. What tools, languages and platforms are used most and how are those capabilities trending over time?

What is Stage

Stage is Booz Allen’s framework to help enable an "InnerSourceTM culture" through Intelligent Discovery of Reusable Assets

  • Leverage our culture of collaboration to inner source innovation, harvesting and exposing capabilities and intellectual capital across the Firm.
  • Maximize investments (Firm and client) to rapidly innovate, curate, and deliver capabilities through reuse.
  • Institutionalize a culture shift of “reuse and building for reuse” to remove geographic and market barriers.

Features of Stage

  • 1

    Intelligent Discovery of Code

    Using a simple search, discover resuable technical assets that can be assembled to accelerate development efforts.

  • 2

    A Framework for Enterprise Software

    To publish, discover, and reuse software and services, driving standardization.

  • 3

    An Intuitive UI

    That aggregates repositories and simplifies access to security frameworks, system blueprints and application stacks.

  • 4

    Guidance on Best Practices

    For developing reusable software components to support goal to reach CMMi Level 5.
