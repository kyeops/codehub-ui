What is Stage

Stage is Booz Allen’s framework to help enable an “Inner Source culture” through Intelligent Discovery of Reusable Assets

  • check Leverage our culture of collaboration to inner source innovation, harvesting and exposing capabilities and intellectual capital across the Firm.
  • check Maximize investments (Firm and client) to rapidly innovate, curate, and deliver capabilities through reuse.
  • check Institutionalize a culture shift of “reuse and building for reuse” to remove geographic and market barriers.
