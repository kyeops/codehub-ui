What is Stage

Stage is Booz Allen’s framework to help enable an "InnerSourceTM culture" through Intelligent Discovery of Reusable Assets

  • Leverage our culture of collaboration to inner source innovation, harvesting and exposing capabilities and intellectual capital across the Firm.
  • Maximize investments (Firm and client) to rapidly innovate, curate, and deliver capabilities through reuse.
  • Institutionalize a culture shift of “reuse and building for reuse” to remove geographic and market barriers.

Features of Stage

  • 1

    Intelligent Discovery of Code

    Using a simple search, discover resuable technical assets that can be assembled to accelerate development efforts.

  • 2

    A Framework for Enterprise Software

    To publish, discover, and reuse software and services, driving standardization.

  • 3

    An Intuitive UI

    That aggregates repositories and simplifies access to security frameworks, system blueprints and application stacks.

  • 4

    Guidance on Best Practices

    For developing reusable software components to support goal to reach CMMi Level 5.
